Registry

We aren’t having a registry because we don’t need no more STUFF! Plus, if you’re already driving, flying, or walking all the way to West Virginia for the wedding, then that’s huge… so thank you!

If you’ve already set aside some of your disposable income to share and share alike, then please consider purchasing a t-shirt from Sweet Potato Pepper. (Maybe you’ve heard of them?) 100% of the profits go to helping homeless dogs and cats!

You can also follow or donate to one of the animal rescues listed below — they’re all great!

  1. Sean Casey Animal Rescue takes in rescued, confiscated, neglected, injured, ill, unmanageable, or otherwise unwanted animals from private owners, zoos, shelters, and other public organizations.
  2. Rebound Hounds Rescue believes that there’s a great home for every dog. How to make that happen is the real work of rescue. We’re committed to bringing dogs and guardians together who are right for each other. We do it by knowing the dog, and knowing the prospective guardian.
  3. Badass Brooklyn Animal Rescue is a 501(c)3 non-profit, all-volunteer, all-breed dog rescue composed of a network of fosters and volunteers who work together to rescue sweet, loving, adoptable dogs from high kill, rural shelters in the southern U.S. states and get them adopted into wonderful forever homes.
  4. Survivor Tails Animal Rescue is a non-profit, all-volunteer organization that exists to rescue, rehome, and rehabilitate homeless, unwanted, abused, and neglected dogs and cats and place them in loving and responsible forever homes.
  5. Zani’s Furry Friends is where our Tater, the dog model, is from! Zani’s is committed to rescuing companion animals from New York City shelters where, unfortunately, they are at significant risk for euthanasia due to overcrowding.
  6. Second Chance Rescue was formed in 2009 on the firm belief that all animals deserve to be loved and cared for, and no animal should ever be abused, neglected or homeless.
  7. Lucky Puppy Rescue is an incredible organization and is the ONLY 100% Not for Profit retail store in Studio City. They are the sister store of The Poopie Foundation!

Three cheers for homeless dogs and cats!
