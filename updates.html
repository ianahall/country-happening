What exactly is a “Country Happening”?

April 26, 2015

What exactly is a “Country Happening” you ask?

Well, the title was inspired by a shindig hosted in 1967 by renowned architect Philip Johnson at his home in Connecticut called the Glass House. The Velvet Underground played, Merce Cunningham’s dance company performed, and Vogue dubbed the event a “Country Happening.”

After taking a tour of the Glass House with Maria and Travis last year, we thought to ourselves “Our wedding will be happening in the country… does that mean that it’s also a Country Happening?” The answer, my friends, is yes.

All of the 1960’s fun from the original Country Happening has been memorialized in archival footage — check it out on Vimeo!
